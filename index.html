About Me

Driven by Creativity, Defined by Experience, Committed to Quality Content 

Driven by Creativity, Defined by Experience, Committed to Quality Content 

Driven by Creativity, Defined by Experience, Committed to Quality Content 

I started my creative journey as a graphic designer during college. The more I created, the more I got pulled toward video editing — the storytelling, the pacing, the energy of a well-cut clip. I taught myself through YouTube, grinding through tutorials until the skills stuck. That led to an internship, then a full-time role, and now I am doing freelance video editing — helping businesses and founders turn raw footage into premium videos that builds real authority online. When I'm not editing, you'll find me on the cricket field — same focus, different game.
